    Class Info

    I am certified in power Vinyasa, a style of yoga where movement is sequenced with breath, and branches from the traditional yoga method of Ashtanga. Fluidity between postures helps quiet the mind and focus on the present moment. While 'power' Vinyasa insinuates vigorous movement and strength building, vinyasa can still be practiced by all levels, ages, and body types! Through our interactions I adapt each class to your level while being mindful of the flow.

    I am currently offering private lessons Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays.

    *I may be available some evenings on other weekdays depending on location.

    Class duration is typically 1 hour. Class times may be modified depending on your schedule (pricing subject to change).

    Music

    I believe music is a key component to physical movement that can elevate your entire practice. I create my own playlists that I find best marry the beat with the energy level of the class. However, I know each client is different! If you have any specific music requests I'd be happy to curate a playlist specifically for you during our session. If you would prefer to play your own music I take no offense! Do what makes you most comfortable!
